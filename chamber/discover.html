About the Area

Lima is the capital city of Peru and one of the most important economic and cultural centers in South America. It offers a mix of historical heritage, modern infrastructure, and diverse business opportunities.

Quick Facts

  • Region: Central Coast of Peru
  • Main Industries: Commerce, tourism, technology, manufacturing

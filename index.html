About Me

My name is Daniel Vilchez, and I am from Peru. I am currently a university student, and I am studying Web Frontend I. In this course, I am learning the fundamentals of web development, including HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.

In this course, I am learning how to build responsive and user-friendly websites. I am especially interested in improving my skills in layout design, styling with CSS, and adding interactivity with JavaScript.

My goal is to continue developing my web development skills and apply what I learn in real projects. I am excited to keep learning, practice consistently, and use this knowledge in future academic and professional opportunities.
